Our Background

American Capital Partners, LLC (“ACP”) is a full-service investment firm founded in 2002. While headquarted in Hauppauge, New York, our firm has four additional offices: New York City, NY; Columbus, GA; Atlanta, GA; and Jacksonville, FL. As a fiduciary, we act in the client’s best interest and provide a foundation for a prudent investment process.

Our Philosophy

Our philosophy is to build strong relationships with our clients and serve each client’s individual needs and objectives with integrity, while providing the highest possible quality advice and personal service in an ever-changing market. While each client has different investment objectives and tolerance for market risk, we at ACP specialize in serving the needs of each client whether you are an aggressive trader or conservative investor.

Our Personnel

At ACP, our registered representatives offer personalized guidance, leveraging their vast experience in assisting institutional investors with portfolio transactions.
